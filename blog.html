MEDBOT

A medical chatbot that provides personalized health advice and guidance to users. The chatbot utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze user queries and provide relevant responses. Additionally, the chatbot allows users to upload medical reports for evaluation and response. This platform is particularly beneficial for women who may feel shy or uncomfortable discussing their health issues with male gynecologists.The proposed chatbot aims to address the issue of users being unaware of treatment options and symptoms for specific diseases. It also seeks to reduce the time and inconvenience associated with hospital visits and telephonic calls. The chatbot's ability to analyze customer sentiments ensures empathetic and supportive responses. By providing personalized health advice and guidance, the proposed chatbot has the potential to improve health outcomes and enhance the overall healthcare experience. The system's effectiveness and user acceptance will be evaluated through a usability study and user feedback analysis
